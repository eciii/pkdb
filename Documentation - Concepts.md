
Types of documentation:

- For reference:
	- Interface documentation (APIs, ABIs, CLIs, IPC interface): formal or semi-formal, concise.
	- Manuals/Handbooks: comprehensive, thus, in general, long.
	- Cheat Sheets: structured, terse and short.
- For learning:
	- Tutorials: favor expeditiousness over comprehensiveness, relies heavily on examples.
	- Guides: concise, summary-like.
